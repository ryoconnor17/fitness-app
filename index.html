Here's how FitForge works
A quick tour so you know exactly where everything is.
1
Dashboard — your home base. See today's workout, weekly volume, streak, and recent activity. Everything at a glance.
2
Log Workout — tap the clock icon to start a session. Log weight, reps, and RPE (effort rating) for each set. Check off sets as you go.
3
Exercise Catalog — browse 12+ exercises with animated technique guides. Tap any card to see coaching cues and your PR.
4
Plans — choose a pre-built program or build your own. Your active plan feeds directly into each day's workout.
5
AI Coach — ask anything. The coach knows your training history and gives personalized, data-driven advice.
